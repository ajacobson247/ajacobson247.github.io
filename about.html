About Me

With 4+ years of app / game development experience in both developer and managerial roles, my focus has heavily revolved on the design and development of virtual and augmented reality applications. I have led projects designed for rehabilitation, training, education, and marketing purposes.

Recently, I led a small team in creating a 3D puzzle-platformer game designed for both Android and iOS tablets for a large educational group. The project has been developed in C++ using Unreal Engine 4 and focuses on reinforcing English grammar concepts for middle school students from migrant families.
