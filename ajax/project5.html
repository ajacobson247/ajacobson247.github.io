App Development

NavNow

NavNow

Project Goal

The goal of the app was to help visitors of the 514,000 sq. ft. Henry B. Gonzalez Convention Center in San Antonio by developing an app that would allow all users to easily navigate their way while inside the building. The app needed to ease the experience of conventions by providing users with custom schedules, internal convention messaging, a convention wide notification system, and privacy settings for each unique convention. The app also included additional augmented reality features that were available to different conventions for custom AR experiences to promote attendee engagement.

  • Category App Development
  • Date 2019
  • Client Henry B Gonzalez Convention Center

Easing the Convention Going Experience

Using React Native, we designed and developed a native app for iOS and Android that utilized Google Firebase to help store and sync user data in realtime. The app allowed users to create and track schedules, message other convention goers, report feedback to the convention or the convention center, receive convention wide notifications, and navigate to any non-restricted area in the building.

We partnered with MapsIndoors, a indoor mapping company based out of Denmark, to map the interior of the Henry B. Gonzalez Convention Center. Since they did not have a ready made solution for React Native at the time, my primary role on the project was to create modules in both Java and Swift that would allow for their API to be integrated into our app.

Additionally, we could not find an augmented reality solution in React Native that met our client's standards. So, similarly, I created native modules that integrated ARKit and ARCore for the iOS version and Android version respectively.
