Dr. Grammar

Dr. Grammar

Project Goal

We were challenged with building an educational video game for Android and IOS. The game would act as supplemental learning in teaching English grammar to 6th through 8th grade migrant students.

We set out to make an engaging open world for our players to explore that used different spatial and physics puzzles and used English grammar puzzles to trigger certain actions, with a narrative involving a mad scientist and you, his robot creation.

  • Category Mobile Game Development
  • Date 2020
  • Client ESC Region 20

Developing an Educational,
Open-world Puzzle Platformer

Developed for both iOS and Android, you play as a robot who awakens in a lab and is asked to help power it back online. Although the driving force behind the game was education, our client wanted students to play it over time and want to keep playing. So, designing a game that young students would enjoy playing and not see as an obligation was important.

The player uses their knowlege of English grammar to solve puzzles, but is also challenged to platform through physical puzzles using the scientist's crazy inventions, such as a gravity machine or motorized raft. The game features 5 unique areas with 15 levels total for the player to explore. Each area builds on the complexity of both the learning objectives and gameplay challenges posed to the player.

The game also has collectible bolts for the player to find as they explore and a narrative that is fully voice acted in both English and Spanish.
